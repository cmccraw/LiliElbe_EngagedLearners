(01) Dear Ed. -@Translator: KEJ HarthernErnst Ludwig Harthern (née Jacobson) (1884–1969), German writer and editor of Man into Woman under the pseudonym, Niels Hoyer; Lili’s German friend in the narrative!

(02) I am hereby sending you what I promised you . .
