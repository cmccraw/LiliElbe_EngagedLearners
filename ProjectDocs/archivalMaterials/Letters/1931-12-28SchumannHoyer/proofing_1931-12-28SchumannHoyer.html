Description of original facsimile: One-page, two-sided typed letter on Carl Reissner stationary in purple (likely a carbon copy) with two holes punched in the lefthand margin and two pairs of two parallel holes in the middle of the letter; a handwritten comment in red crayon by Harthern on the bottom of the page and a red crayon line drawn down the lefthand side of the second page.

(03) I hereby declare, that I have never gained the German rights to a foreign work, without having given an advance payment when the contract was concluded.

(04) Furthermore, I have never given away rights to foreign countries, without having received an advance payment when the contract was concluded.
